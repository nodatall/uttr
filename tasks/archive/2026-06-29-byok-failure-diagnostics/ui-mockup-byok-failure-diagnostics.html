Uttr Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Information we collect

We collect the information needed to run Uttr, including account email, subscription and entitlement status, install or trial identifiers, usage event metadata, short-lived diagnostic metadata, support messages, and basic server logs.

Audio, transcripts, prompts, and diagnostics

Uttr's desktop app is designed to keep local app history on your device. Failure diagnostics are used to detect and fix app problems. Diagnostic events do not include audio, transcripts, prompts, selected text, clipboard contents, API keys, or provider response bodies.

Retention

Raw diagnostic events are kept for about seven days. Account, billing, entitlement, support, usage, and security records are kept for as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations.
