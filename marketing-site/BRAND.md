# Uttr Brand Notes (Marketing Site)

## Logo Variants

- `nav`: app icon + wordmark for sticky navigation.
- `watermark`: oversized low-opacity lockup behind hero content.
- `footer`: compact monochrome lockup.

## Asset Source

- Use the official Tauri build icon from `src-tauri/icons/logo.png`.
- Marketing copy lives at `public/uttr-icon.png`.

## Usage Rules

- Minimum display size:
  - Icon: 24px tall.
  - Wordmark lockup: 140px wide.
- Clear space: keep at least half the icon width around the lockup.
- Contrast: keep logos on dark cosmic surfaces with at least AA contrast for the wordmark.
- Watermark opacity: keep below 0.3 to protect headline readability.

## Palette Alignment

- Primary foreground: `#f5efff`
- Accent pink: `#f6a6da`
- Accent lilac: `#b588ff`
- Accent blue: `#78b2ff`
